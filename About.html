About us

We bring the outdoor movie experience to you with our public events and private sceening's.
The classic drive in movies at our Trafford centre,Etihad satium,Old trafford stadium where we hold a long term Radio licence for the site.
The outdoor walk-in movie experience to you, where we distribute sound at any venue via professional loud speaker system.
Back yard movies where we bring the outdoor cinema action to your garden.
With a massive 9 metre x 7 metre screen for the large events and a more compact 4.5m x 3m for the more intimate occassions,
we bring the outside cinema experience direct to you.
Old Movies, New Movies and Classic Movies will be shown allowing cinema and theatre goers to have fun of the movies under the stars.
We are adding venues and listings to our offers and are open to working with new organisations that wish to host Drive events.

Food&drinks

We Do Winter Drive-In in true style with our fantastic selection of Christmas-themed food and drinks.
So whether you fancy a water or a slushie we will make sure you have everything
you need to immerse yourself in the Christmas cheer. What's more, you can order all these goodies right from
the comfort of your car simply by scanning the QR code on the wireless in-car speaker box you receive as part of our epic Drive-In experience.
Or why not treat yourself to a mixed Box by adding this to your order ahead of time?
Think festive treats such as popcorn,crisps paired with a slushie or even with a can of coke ready for you on arrival to enjoy with the film.
